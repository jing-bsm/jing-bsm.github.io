Our Beneficiaries

For the past 25 years, the PGI’s have raised over to $700,000.00 to support Literacy initiatives and awareness campaigns right here in our own province. 100% of all funds raised are used to support these very worthy causes.

2015 marks the 25th anniversary of the PGI’s in Newfoundland and Labrador, and funds raised this years will be used to support on-going initiatives for the each for the following:

The Community Centre Alliance

Literacy Skills Development & Employment Program

(supporting Community Centre program and services around the province)

College of the North Atlantic

Scholarships and Bursaries for Students of CAS and ABE programs entering the programs offered at the College
